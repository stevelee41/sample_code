Psychiatry

Four elements to establish capacity

1: Communicate a choice

questions you can ask:

Can you tell me what your choice is?

2: Understand relevant information

questions you can ask:

    Can you tell me in your own words:
  • the issue with your health now?
  • the recommended treatment?
  • the possible benefits and risks of this proposed treatment?
  • any alternative treatments along their associated risks and benefits
  • risks and benefits of no treatment

3: Appreciate situation and its consequences

questions you can ask:

  • What do you believe the issue is with your health right now?
  • Do you believe you need some sort of treatment?
  • What is this treatment likely to do for you?
  • What makes you believe this treatment will have this effect?
  • What do you believe will happen of you don't receive treatment?
  • Why do you think your doctor recommended this treatment?

4: Reason rationally

questions you can ask:

  • How did you decide to accept (or reject) recommended treatment?
  • What makes your chosen option better than the alternative?
