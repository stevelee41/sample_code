CVA and TIA

CVA/TIA initial checklist

  • noncontrast CT head
  • aspirin
  • telemetry/cardiac monitor
  • echocardiogram
  • statin
  • hemoglobin A1c
  • blood pressure control
  • carotid ultrasound

Contraindications to tPA

Absolute contraindications

  • acute intracranial hemorrhage
  • history of intracranial hemorrhage
  • severe uncontrolled hypertension (>185/110)
  • serious head trauma or CVA in past 3 months
  • thrombocytopenia (platelets < 100K)
  • coagulopathy
    • INR>1.7
    • PT>15 seconds
  • low molecular weight heparin
  • direct thrombin inhibitors
    • dabigatran
    • argatroban
  • Factor Xa inhibitors
    • apixaban
    • rivaroxaban
  • severe hypoglycemia (controversial)

    • glucose < 50

  • early radiographic ischemic changes (controversial)

Relative contraindications

  • mild/improving CVA symptoms
  • severe CVA and coma
  • recent major surgery
  • arterial puncture of noncompressible vessel
  • recent GI/GU hemorrhage
  • seizure at onset of CVA symptoms
  • recent MI
  • CNS structure lesions
    • neoplasm
    • AVM
    • aneurism
  • dementia
