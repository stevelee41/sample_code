Vent alarms

high pressure alarm

  • secretions, need to suction (common)
  • water in tubing (common)
  • kinked tubing, malpositioned ETT
  • patient biting tube, fighting vent
  • bronchospasm
  • decreased lung compliance (ARDS)

Hypertensive urgencies and emergencies

defined: SBP > 180 mm Hg and/or DBP > 120 mm Hg

reduce BP by maximum of 25% over the first hour

then reduce BP to 160/(100-110) mm Hg over the next 2-6 hours

next reduce to normal over next 24-48 hours
